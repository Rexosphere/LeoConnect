# Design Tips for Slides

## Visual Guidelines

1. **Color Palette**: 
   - Primary: #000000 (Black)
   - Accent: #06B6D4 (Teal/Cyan)
   - Background: #FFFFFF or #0A0A0A

2. **Fonts**:
   - Headings: Inter Bold or Poppins
   - Body: Inter Regular

3. **Keep it Minimal**:
   - Max 6 bullet points per slide
   - More visuals, less text
   - Let the demo speak

4. **Include**:
   - App screenshots
   - Architecture diagram
   - Team photo (optional)
